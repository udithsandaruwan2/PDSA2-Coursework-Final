Game Rules

  • Players alternate placing O and X on the 5×5 board.
  • Player O always starts first.
  • The goal is to get 5 of your marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • If the board fills without 5 in a row, the game ends in a draw.
  • Select your AI algorithm before starting: Minimax or MCTS.
